Welcome

In order to use Breadcrumbs, you'll need to get a Yelp API key. Don't worry - we won't save it to our servers! We'll save it to your computer so that anytime you access Breadcrumbs from your computer, you won't have to input this information again. We use your key to access the Yelp API. Providing your key will be understood as your consent to use the key to perform your searches against the Yelp database and populate categories.

Breadcrumbs: How It Works

Hello and welcome to Breadcrumbs! Breadcrumbs is an app designed to help you bookmark and organize businesses you’ve found on Yelp. The name “Breadcrumbs” should make you think of a trail of crumbs along a path, guiding you towards your favorite restaurants and bars. Breadcrumbs’s simple organization and streamlined design will make it easy for you to get the most out of Yelp’s deep database of businesses. To add crumbs to Breadcrumbs: go to "Add Crumbs", either browse the categories or enter a search term, find the crumb you want to add, and click on its add crumb icon. To make a new list: go to "My Crumbs", select "Add List", and type in the list name. To add a crumb to a list: find the crumb in "All Crumbs", click on its list icon, and check the box next to the list you want to add the crumb to. To remove a crumb from Breadcrumbs: find the crumb in any list, click on its list icon, and uncheck the box next to “All Crumbs”.

About

Breadcrumbs in a project created by Jonathan Balsano, Logan Donovan, Mark Micchelli, and Yanyi Luo. It was done as the final project for the COMS W4170: User Interface Design class at Columbia University in the Fall of 2012. This project serves as a functional proof of concept and is not intended to be a usable application.
